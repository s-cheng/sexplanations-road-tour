Tour Dates

Since November there have been dozens of us working on setting up venues for the Sexplanations Road Tour to sex educate. We've sent messages to over 500 libraries, emailed and called 100s of coffee shops, dozens of universities, sex stores, bookstores, restaurants, and communities centers.
The response has often been "we can't have that here" or "that's too out of the box for us." So everyone working on this has relied almost entirely on connections made by fans onsite. And we've had to be really creative. We've met with audiences in a back room of a theater, the social area of a church, the parking lot at a human rights memorial, a grocery store cafe, a mall, and outside a police station. We're trying!
If there is a * next to the location listed below this means a venue and time have not been determined. We will still go to the city but there isn't an official stop yet.
To know where we'll be or for any last minute changes, check Twitter (@elleteedee).
You're also welcome to make connections to safe spaces by emailing helpdrdoe@gmail.com.
